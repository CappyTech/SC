Server Rules and Offense Tiers

Offense Tiers and Consequences

Minor Offenses

  1. 1st Offense: Warning
  2. 2nd Offense: Day Ban
  3. 3rd Offense: Week Ban
  4. 4th Offense: Permanent Ban

Moderate Offenses

  1. 1st Offense: Warning or Day Ban
  2. 2nd Offense: Week Ban
  3. 3rd Offense: Permanent Ban

Severe Offenses

  1. 1st Offense: Week or Month Ban
  2. 2nd Offense: Permanent Ban

Atrocious Offenses

  1. 1st Offense: Immediate Permanent Ban

Basic Rules

Summary: This server is designed to be a fun and educational environment. Our primary goal is education, and our secondary goal is to foster community interaction and building. Violating these principles may result in a ban. Remember, using this server is a privilege, not a right.
